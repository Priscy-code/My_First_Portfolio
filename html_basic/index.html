Welcome to alx training program

bringing the best out of yourself

ALX is a leading training and talent placement platform focused on equipping young Africans with the skills needed to thrive in the digital age.

It's part of the African Leadership Group (ALG), a larger ecosystem dedicated to developing and connecting young leaders in Africa. 2

ALX specializes in training individuals in in-demand tech skills such as software engineering, DevOps, and data science. Their curriculum is designed to be practical and industry-relevant.

Beyond technical skills, ALX focuses on developing leadership qualities and soft skills essential for career success.
